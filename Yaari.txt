# Yaari
Part 1: The Journey to the village
Prathi Samvasharam dussera holidays ki village Vache Ganesh(hero) eppatilaage thana friend (arun)  ni ventabettukoni bus lo Hyderabad nundi villlage ki bayalderadu.  Ganesh bus window seat lo kurchunadu, Arun thana pakkana kuurchuni conductor tho comedy chesthu bus lo andarini navvisthunnadu. Adhi chusi Ganesh thana manasulo thana chinnnapati friend ni thaluchukuntu thana manaslo thanu matladukuntunnadu “Vaadu kuda ilane thana pakkana unnavarini eppadiki navisthu, navvuthu undevaadu. Nenu eppudu moodiga unna vaade edhoti chesi na badha pogottevaadu” anukuntu ala nidhraloki jari thana chinnanaati gathanni gurthu chesukunnadu.

Flashback 1:
Ganesh valla dady urlo andhariki baaga thelisinavaadu and koncham palukubadi undhi, dhani valla thana intlo koncham thanu ante andhariki chala bayam, thanu kuda discipline, rules ani strict ga unde vaadu. Ayana dhebbaki assalu ganesh tho evaru  friendship chesinaa okati,leka rendu rojulake valla nana dhebbaki chiraakesi vadhilesevallu. Ganesh ki valla nana medha entha kopam unna cheppaleka thanalo thane badha padevaadu. Appudu kalisaadu mana bulli krishnudu andhru vadini mudhugaa “chintu ani pilustharu” (so manam kuda alane pichukundham). Roju laage school bus ekkina Ganesh window seat lo kuchunnadu, thana tho matladaniki evaru leka poyesariki chala modiga okkade badha paduthunnadu. Thana pakkane kurchunna chintu thanu moodiga undatam chusi,


Andaririni navvinnchadu. Ala valla iddharu okariki okaru parichayam chesukoni frds ayyaru. 

Inthalo oka pedha speed break kudhuputhoo nidhralechina ganesh , thana shoulder pina padukuna arun chusi navvukunnadu. Pakkane arun nidhralo atu etu uguthunna arun thana shoulder paina pettu kunnadu. Kaasepu bus tea thagadam kosam bus aagindhi. Canteen lo tea thaguthu arun and ganesh matladukuntunnaru.
